Professional Ventures

Working with wonderful people and products to achieve, grow, and deliver.

  • Professional

Treyarch/Volt – Call of Duty: Black Ops 4

In June 2017, I began my journey at Treyarch with the amazing development team dedicated to making Call of Duty: Black Ops 4. I worked closest with the Multiplayer Maps Development team, joining forces with an array of disciplines to bring the Black Ops multiplayer world to life. Beginning with the earliest maps and continuing through our DLC season, I dedicated my efforts to delivering cohesive, high-quality, and performant maps to players around the globe across major platforms (PS4/Xbox/PC).

As our development team moves onto new projects, I will further strive to generate a strong, streamlined, and agile team with my production partners – ready to deliver the best our studio has to offer.

Outside of work, you can catch me in the game on PS4 or PC, or lurking on our community postings, snatching up player feedback to help generate action items for MP maps present and future – targeting improved gameplay, usability, and content.

WG Cells (Dropforge Games) – Smash Squad

Starting in May 2016, I began work as a technical writer for WG Cells (formerly known as Dropforge games, prior to being acquired by Wargaming). This was my first industry experience!

While there, I re-established and managed the entirety of the company’s JIRA/Confluence documentation base. Through the creation of streamlined documentation protocols and templates, the following became realities for the studio:

  • Widespread document organization
  • Concise and easily accessible information
  • Cohesive and consistent presentation of studio knowledge
Additionally, I generated new documentation for on-going and upcoming studio projects – including the studio’s final mobile releases Operation: New Earth and Smash Squad.

Unfortunately, WG Cells closed its doors in September 2016, thus marking the end of my employment with the fine people there. Since then, we have all moved on to do equally amazing things both in and outside of the industry.
