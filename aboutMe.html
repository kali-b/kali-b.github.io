Summary

I am a dedicated producer, who draws on experience from many facets of life and our industry, ranging from student explorations, to mobile development, to AAA development, to personal projects.

Currently, I am employed with Treyarch as a Production Coordinator in Los Angeles, CA. June 26th, 2019 will mark my first 2 years in the AAA industry. My areas of focus are:

  • Content Production (Level Design/Environment Art)
  • Team Advocacy and Management
  • Consumer Advocacy
  • Product Management
  • Product Usability and Performance
  • Product Testing

I harbor a firm belief that a happy team and polished product will best satisfy a consumer’s wants and needs. For me, there is nothing more rewarding than seeing a game idea born and carried out to conclusion. I strive to enable a team to make the most of their vision and development time.
Production is the essence of my soul, supported by a strong foundation in technology and the arts. Assisting a team, managing a product, and serving high-quality experiences to players is my blood.
