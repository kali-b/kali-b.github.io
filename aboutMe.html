About Me

Summary

I am a dedicated producer, drawing on experience from many facets of life and our industry: ranging from student explorations, to mobile development, to AAA development, to personal projects.

Currently, I am employed with Treyarch as a Production Coordinator in Los Angeles, CA -- and June of this year will mark two exiting years of employment with this fantastic studio. My areas of focus are:

  • Content Production (Level/Systems Design and Environment Art)
  • Technical Production
  • Team Advocacy and Management
  • Consumer Advocacy
  • Product Management
  • Game Performance
  • Product Usability/Testing

I harbor a firm belief that a happy team and polished product will best satisfy a consumer’s wants and needs. For me, there is nothing more rewarding than seeing a game idea born and carried out to conclusion. I strive to enable a team to make the most of their vision and development time.

Production is the essence of my soul, supported by a strong foundation in technology and the arts. Assisting a team, managing a product, and serving high-quality experiences to players is my blood.

Production and Technical Knowledge

Along my academic and professional journey in games, I have been alotted great opportunities to hone and develop several technical, intrapersonal, and production management skills with a wide array of project types and people.

To begin with technical, I am most proficient in the C++ language, having extensive knowledge of the ISO 98, and ISO 11 standards. It follows that I am comfortable with the finer points of:

  • Memory Management
  • Object-oriented Programming
  • Polymorphism
  • Class and Code Structure
  • Pointer Arithmetic
  • Code Optimization
  • Low-level Operations

My second most proficient language is C, being familiar with the C89, C90, and C99 standards. Thus, it follows that I am also capable of strictly procedural programming approaches. In addition to C++ and C, I have also had exposure to/worked with/studied the following languages and libraries:

  • C#
  • FMOD
  • Python
  • Java
  • JavaScript
  • HTML/CSS
  • Markdown
  • D
  • GLSL (OpenGL shader language)
  • Assembly
  • XInput
  • SDL
  • Nodejs
  • LUA

I have programmed and developed custom-built game engines, and used pre-built engines (Unity 5/Unreal 4) in both Windows and Linux environments. Additionally, I have developed prototypes and a virtual-reality gaming experience for the HTC Vive.

Utilizing JavaScript, I have created Google Apps integrations to develop structured workflows and facilitate team-progress documentation through automated tools. I also have experience with the following project management tools:

  • Atlassian Tools
  • Shotgun Software
  • Microsoft Office
  • Slack
  • Perforce
  • ...and several more.

These programs and tools have slotted into various blends of production ideologies I have spear-headed on teams. Though I would consider Agile and SCRUM to be my favored production methods, I have also worked with aspects of Waterfall, Kanban, XP, and Spiral Development.

In an ever-growing industry, there is always more to learn and achieve for managing unique situations. I am confident in my abilities to establish and discover production methods that can work for a product and team. It is what I continuously strive for every day.
