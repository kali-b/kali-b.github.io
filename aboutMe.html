Professional Summary

Hello there! Currently, I am living in Los Angeles and working on Call of Duty as a Senior Associate Producer at Treyarch. I am dedicated to gathering information and inspiration from all walks of life, using this knowledge to remain a positive force on my teams. June 2021 marks my 4th year in the exciting world of AAA games. My areas of focus are:

  • Content Production (World Building, Design, and Art)
  • Technical Production
  • Team Advocacy, Communication, and Management
  • Consumer Advocacy
  • Product Management
  • Game Performance
  • Product Usability/Testing/Stability

I harbor a firm belief that happy teams deliver polished products, making players happy in turn. For me, there is nothing more rewarding than seeing a game idea born and carried through to said polish. I strive to enable teams to make the most of their vision and development time.

Production is the essence of my soul, supported by a strong foundation in technology and the arts. Assisting a team, managing a product, and serving high-quality experiences to players - it's in my blood.

Production and Technical Knowledge

Along my journey in games, I have been alotted great opportunities to hone and develop technical, intrapersonal, and production management skills in various settings.

To briefly outline my technical roots: I am most proficient in the C++ (ISO98, ISO11), and C (C89, C90, C99) programming languages. In addition to C++ and C, I have also had exposure to/worked with the following languages and libraries:

  • C#
  • FMOD
  • Python
  • Java
  • JavaScript
  • HTML/CSS
  • Markdown
  • D
  • GLSL (OpenGL shader language)
  • Assembly
  • XInput
  • SDL
  • Nodejs
  • LUA

I have programmed and developed custom-built game engines, and have also worked with pre-built engines (Unity 5/Unreal 4) in both Windows and Linux. Additionally, I have developed mobile prototypes, a virtual-reality gaming experience for the HTC Vive, and have produced technical aspects of AAA games that have launched on PC, Xbox, and PS4.

Alongside my existing technical training, I still dabble in and enjoy working in/around the following:

  • Maya
  • PhotoShop
  • Radiant
  • Web Design
  • Interface Design
  • Game System/Mechnanics Design
  • World and Map Building
  • Game Storytelling

My technical and creative knowledge equipped me to begin my foray into games production. I have worked with technical, creative, and publishing teams to discover production solutions in changing and challenging envrionments. As our industry and players grow and change every year, it makes sense that our approach to games should too. I have taken a great amount of time to study Agile production methods - blending elements of SCRUM, Kanban, and others into pipelines for successful teams and products. Here are some of the software tools I have used to make this happen:

  • JIRA
  • Confluence
  • Slack
  • HipChat
  • Asana
  • Microsoft Office
  • Google Office Suite
  • Source Control (Perforce, Github, SVN, etc.)

Games are truly my passion. I love the working environment, and the joy/community we bring to players. Hitting the books has been instrumental to my experience and ability, but the real growth happens in the trenches with devs, in showrooms with players, and whenever I boot up a game in my own living room.
