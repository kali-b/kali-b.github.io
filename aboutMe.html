Professional Summary

Hello there! At the moment, I am living in Los Angeles and working on Call of Duty as an Associate Producer at Treyarch. I am dedicated to constantly gathering information and inspiration from all walks of life. I use this knowledge to remain a positive force on my teams, driving my work and our games to success. June 2020 marks my 3rd year in the exciting world of AAA games. My areas of focus are:

  • Content Production (Level/Systems Design and Environment Art)
  • Technical Production
  • Team Advocacy, Communication, and Management
  • Consumer Advocacy
  • Product Management
  • Game Performance
  • Product Usability/Testing

I harbor a firm belief that a happy team will deliver a polished product, making players happy in turn. For me, there is nothing more rewarding than seeing a game idea born and shipped. I strive to enable teams to make the most of their vision and development time.

Production is the essence of my soul, supported by a strong foundation in technology and the arts. Assisting a team, managing a product, and serving high-quality, creative experiences to players is my blood.

Production and Technical Knowledge

Along my academic and professional journey in games, I have been alotted great opportunities to hone and develop several technical, intrapersonal, and production management skills with a wide array of project types and people.

To begin with technical, I am most proficient in the C++ language, familiar with both the ISO 98 and ISO 11 standards. My second most proficient language is C (C89, C90, and C99 standards). In addition to C++ and C, I have also had exposure to/worked with the following languages and libraries:

  • C#
  • FMOD
  • Python
  • Java
  • JavaScript
  • HTML/CSS
  • Markdown
  • D
  • GLSL (OpenGL shader language)
  • Assembly
  • XInput
  • SDL
  • Nodejs
  • LUA

I have programmed and developed custom-built game engines, and also used pre-built engines (Unity 5/Unreal 4) in both Windows and Linux. Additionally, I have developed mobile prototypes, a virtual-reality gaming experience for the HTC Vive, and AAA games that have launched on PC, Xbox, and PS4.

Alongside my technical training, I have also been awarded great opportunity to work with strong creative individuals, expanding my horizons further. I dabble in and enjoy working in/around the following:

  • Maya
  • PhotoShop
  • Radiant
  • Web Design
  • Interface Design
  • Game System/Mechnanics Design
  • World and Map Building
  • Game Storytelling

My technical and creative knowledge equipped me with what I needed to begin my foray into games production. I have worked with technical, creative, and publishing teams to discover production solutions to an array of challenges in development. I thrive in an environment of change. As our industry and players grow and change every year, it makes sense that our approach to games should too. I have taken a great amount of time to study Agile production methods -- blending elements of SCRUM, Kanban, and even "non-Agile" elements into pipelines which suit my teams and our products. Here are some of the software tools I have used to make this happen:

  • JIRA
  • Confluence
  • Slack
  • HipChat
  • Asana
  • Microsoft Office
  • Google Office Suite
  • Source Control (Perforce, Github, SVN, etc.)

Games are truly my passion. I love the environment they create, and the joy and community they bring to players alongside those who make them. Hitting the books and papers has been instrumental to my experience and ability, but the real growth happens in the trenches with devs, in showrooms with players, and whenever I boot up a game in my own living room.
